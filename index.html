FAQ

Questions We Often Hear

Everything you need to know before booking your appointment at Sri Rekha Beauty Parlour, Bhainsa.

Do you offer bridal packages for weddings in Bhainsa and Nirmal district? +
Yes! We offer Silver, Gold, and Royal bridal packages suited to all budgets. We cater to weddings across Bhainsa, Nirmal, Mudhole, Khanapur, and surrounding areas in Telangana and Maharashtra border. Book at least 2–3 months in advance for bridal slots.
What is the pre-bridal package and when should I start? +
Our pre-bridal package includes a series of facials, clean-up, de-tan, and skin nourishment treatments designed to give your skin a natural glow. Ideally start 3–6 months before the wedding for the best results. Even a 1-month package shows visible improvement.
Do you provide a trial makeup session before the wedding? +
Absolutely. We strongly recommend a trial makeup session 2–3 weeks before your wedding day. This helps us understand your preferences, test products on your skin, and ensure the final look is exactly what you envisioned.
What makeup brands and products do you use? +
We use professional, certified beauty brands known for their longevity and skin safety. All products are skin-tested, dermatologically appropriate, and suitable for Indian skin tones. We never compromise on product quality.
Can you do makeup and services at my home or venue? +
Yes! We offer doorstep services for bridal clients and group bookings. Home visit charges may apply depending on the distance. Please call or WhatsApp us to confirm availability for your date and location.
How do I book an appointment at Sri Rekha Beauty Parlour? +
You can book by calling us, sending a WhatsApp message, or filling the enquiry form on this page. For bridal bookings, we recommend booking 2–3 months in advance as slots fill quickly, especially during wedding season.
Do you offer services for men or only women? +
Sri Rekha Beauty Parlour is a women-only beauty salon. We serve women and girls of all ages, from young girls to senior ladies. We offer family-friendly packages for wedding groups including mothers and sisters of the bride.
What are your working hours and location? +
We are open daily from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are located in Bhainsa, Nirmal District, Telangana – 504103. Please call ahead to confirm availability and avoid waiting. Walk-ins are welcome, but appointments are preferred.
